Concerned about Trent's new vaccination policy?

You are not alone.

We are a group of a students from Trent University who believe that vaccination is a personal medical decision to be made without compulsion.

We consider Trent's recent decision to require students to dislose their vaccination status and treat them differently because of it to be intrusive, unscientific and grossly unethical.

We completely oppose any effort on part of Trent to mandate that all students be vaccinated im order to access campus.

We fully empathize with the need to keep everyone safe at this time while advocating that this be done in a way that is minimally restrictive on the rights of students.

Our goal is to unite liberty-minded Trent students under one banner to stand against these sorts of attacks on our freedoms. Contact us today!
